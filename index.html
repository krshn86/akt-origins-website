About Us
A
"We believe people deserve to know the truth about what they buy. And brands deserve to prove it."

AKT-ORIGINS builds Digital Product Passports for any product that touches the EU market. It doesn't matter where a product is made. If it's sold, imported, or traded within the EU, it needs a verified digital identity. We create that identity, helping brands protect themselves from counterfeits, helping regulators do their job, and helping everyday consumers know exactly what they're buying.

A Passport for Every Product

Any product entering or leaving the EU will need a Digital Product Passport. We build that passport — a verified digital identity that travels with the product through its entire life.

02

Brand Protection Built In

Counterfeits damage brands and mislead customers. Our passports make it easy to prove what's real and what's not — giving brands a practical tool to protect what they've built.

03

Transparency for Consumers

People deserve to know what they're buying: where it came from, what it's made of, and whether it's genuine. We put that information directly in their hands.
